Not receiving your OTP?

Try these quick checks before reaching out for help — most OTP delivery issues are resolved in a minute.

Step01

Confirm your mobile network signal

Check that your phone has cellular signal. If the signal bar is empty or showing only one bar, move to an open area or near a window and try again. Wi-Fi alone is not enough — SMS delivery requires an active cellular connection.

Step02

Verify your SIM is active and able to receive SMS

Ask someone to send you a regular SMS on the same number. If you do not receive their message either, your SIM may be inactive, recently ported, or set to block incoming SMS. Contact your telecom operator to check the status of your SIM.

Step03

Check your SMS spam or blocked folder

Some phones automatically route OTP and other transactional messages to a separate spam, promotional, or blocked folder. Open your Messages app, switch to that tab, and look for messages from Punch.

Step04

Toggle Airplane Mode

Turn Airplane Mode on, wait 10 seconds, then turn it off. This forces your phone to reconnect to the cellular network and often releases SMS that were stuck in transit. Once reconnected, request a new OTP.
